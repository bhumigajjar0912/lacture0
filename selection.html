In this solution we have created a alumni portal for the Goa University
in which for we have various features like an alumni can register itself
and update their details which will lead them to create their portfolios.
Except that, for alumni there are various options like newsletters and
events details of their colleges, group chat with their mates and friends.
In addition to this there is also a chat box for the interaction of students
and alumni. Plus the alumni can get regular email and messages of the next alumni events.
